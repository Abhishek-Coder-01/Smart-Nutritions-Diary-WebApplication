Understanding Nutrition Tracking

Learn how to make the most of your nutrition journey

Macronutrients

Track proteins, carbohydrates, and fats - the building blocks of your diet that provide energy and support bodily functions.

  • Proteins: Muscle repair and growth
  • Carbs: Primary energy source
  • Fats: Energy storage and hormone production

Micronutrients

Monitor essential vitamins and minerals that support immune function, bone health, and metabolic processes.

  • Vitamins: A, C, D, E, K, B-complex
  • Minerals: Calcium, Iron, Magnesium, Zinc
  • Electrolytes: Sodium, Potassium
